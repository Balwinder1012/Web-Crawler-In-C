Placement

List of JMIT Toppers

With many first of its credit and an impeccable record to boast of, the T&P department works round the clock to ensure that students of the institute get placed in reputed companies on lucrative terms. The training and placement department works relentlessly towards the ultimate goal of students’ employment across various industries, or to help them begin a business venture in which they can achieve phenomenal success.

The department is operated with a twin-fold focus, i.e. augmenting internal competencies by contemporary grooming of students and by enabling the industry to identify and absorb intellectuals with requisite technical, managed skills. The staunch corporate patronage which Mukand Institutes enjoys, and which separates it from its contemporaries, originates from its unequivocal focus to produce world class technocrats & managers for which the regular corporate interactions weave the thread.

Placement process aims to match the requirement of the recruiter and the budding aspirations of the students by undermentioned tasks/ events:
# Personality analysis through personality development programs
# Gap identifications
# Personality grooming and skills enhancement
# Role plays/ Practice session
# Mock Interviews
# Pre-placement talk and career counselling
# CV formation
# Live Projects(Industry requirement oriented)

JMIT, Radaur, is a Self Financing Engineering ISO certified 9001-2008 Institute and accredited by TCS , run on No Profit – No Loss basis offering B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and MBA courses in the State of Haryana; affiliated to Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra.

The Institute is well equipped with Qualified Staff, Workshops & Laboratories, a fully Computerized Modern Library, a State-of- Art Information Technology infrastructure including over 850 computer systems backed up with licensed software for all curriculum and training needs including high speed internet facility.

Students are admitted on all India merit ranking through JEE written test only for B.Tech, GATE score for M.Tech., MAT score for MBA and common Haryana test for MCA. All seats are filled as per Haryana Govt./Kurukshetra University guidelines issued from time to time. JMIT is not charging any extra fees for the management seats in all courses offered in the Institute and all seats are open to merit on ALL INDIA BASIS. This attracts high ranking students from all over India.

JMIT take this opportunity to invite the Perspective Employers to JMIT, Radaur for Campus recruitments and Industrial Trainings of B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and MBA students.
