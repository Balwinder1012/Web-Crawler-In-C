M.B.A

The secret of the spectacular growth of Seth Jai Parkash Mukand Lal Institute of Engineering and Technology perhaps lies in its commitment to providing world-class education, more than just business management. The commitment is thought leadership with a deep understanding of business. The approach to pedagogy combines fieldwork, case studies and instrumented feedback with a strong emphasis on concepts and theory. A state of the art infrastructure provides the environment for nurturing talent and for empowering the students with the knowledge, skills and mindset those together shape future business leaders.

2-Years Master Programme in Management (in 3 Specializations)

Following Specializations are offered in MBA

  • Marketing
  • Human Resource Management
  • Finance

The Business Programme is market-oriented and meets the challenges of Modern Management, Trade & Industry needs, and above all the Leadership at Entrepreneur and Corporate Level. These develop subject- specific as well as soft skills which help students to have an early head start for a rewarding career.

JMIT has a separate Placement Cell comprising of Placement Officer, Placement Coordinator and Students Representatives who are diligently working for the Training and Placement activities.
