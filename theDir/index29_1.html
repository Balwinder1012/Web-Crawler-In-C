Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation

  • Overview
  • Curriculum
  • Labs
  • Academic Activities
  • Thrust Areas
  • Admission Info

M.M.I.P.R is one of the esteemed Institute in the field of Physiotherapy which was established in 2001, in the state of Haryana and is vital word in the lexicon of the Maharishi Markandeshwar University. The MM Institute of Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation train efficient professionals in the art of relieving human pain and suffering, using the evidence based practice in the field of physiotherapy, latest skill and instruments coming from the most advanced centres of quality production.
The institute is fully equipped with the ultramodern Research labs with ultramodern research equipments. The institute also has departmental library which is fully supported with Internet connection, Journals, Archives and surplus books for academic up gradation. The institute is recognized by the Indian Association of Physiotherapists (IAP).

The Institute excels in producing fully trained physiotherapists to meet the growing demand in the global market. The clinical skills of the students are nurtured by the postings of the students in our fully equipped 815 bedded M. M. Medical College & Hospital. Having achieved a remarkable progress in a short span, the Institute has attained the status of a Centre of Excellence by introducing Post Graduate courses of MPT in five specialties’ of Musculoskeletal Disorders, Neurological Disorders, Cardio-Thoracic Disorders, Sports Injuries, Pediatric Disorders. In 2016 our institute introduced PhD in Physiotherapy programme.

Anatomy

The study of anatomy will include identification of all gross anatomical structures. Particulars emphasis will be placed on description of bones, joints, muscles, the brain, cardio pulmonary and nervous system, as these are related to the application of physiotherapy and occupational therapy in patients.

 

Physiology

Assist the students to acquire knowledge of the normalphysiology of various body systems and understand the alternation in physiology in disease and practice of Physiotherapy as applicable for each systemic disorder.

 

Biochemistry

To acquire knowledge of various concepts of Bio-molecules and their significance in human body.

 

 

Sociology

To understand the uses and science of society and also the application of knowledge of sociology in Physiotherapy practices. 

 

General & clinical psychology

 

Provide basic knowledge about human psychology and its importance in physiotherapy which helps in treatment techniques.

 

Basic principles in physiotherapy

Provide basic knowledge about application of general mechanics and physical agents in physiotherapy.

English.

 

Increase Language awareness to assist in proof reading, research and project work in physiotherapy.
