MMICT & BM has earned its strong academic reputation. Since it’s founding in 1999, the Institute is providing comprehensive and collaborative student-centred learning environment for computer science and applications and professional values associated with the discipline.

An industry-oriented curriculum is the designed to beat industry requirements. It also equips students with skills and provides them with experiences necessary to be able to work successfully in the present sophisticated computing environment. It helps students to become responsible IT professionals with strong moral values.

Our faculty members are highly diverse and unconventional team of experts who find ways to work across disciplines to tackle different challenges. Apart from the teaching, faculty members are continuously involved in various research activities. More than 300 research papers have been published by the faculty and research scholars in National/International Journals or Conferences.

In order to improve the programming skills of the students, Institute offers opportunities for students to develop live projects under the supervision of the faculty. To update knowledge about the recent advancements and needs of the IT industry various Industrial Visits, Technical Workshops, Expert Talks and Entrepreneurship Development Camp are conducted frequently. For overall development of the students various co-curricular and sports activities are organized frequently. Most of our Alumni are well settled throughout the globe.

Key Features
  • Institute is committed to beat industry requirement by exploring the students in Java Programming, Cloud Computing, Mobile Apps Development using Android, iOS Programming using Objective-C, Business Intelligence, Big Data Analytics etc. 
  • Institute also offers opportunities to its students under the guidance of faculty to work on live projects. Some of the successfully completed live projects are FM Radio All India Stations-A Mobile App, DigiBration and musiclamhe Websites, UGC prescribed API System, Online Feedback Systems, Pre-placement Online Mock test, Cricket Score Board etc.
  • Our highly experienced faculty has been involved in various research activities and serves as Keynote Speakers, Session Chair and Advisory Board Members at various International/National Conferences. 
  • Events are organised on the latest Technological Developments, Industrial visits, Technical Workshops, Expert Talks, Personality Development Programme, Entrepreneurship Development Camp, etc.
  • Various social activities like Blood Donation, Clothes Donation, Swachta Abhiyan, Mission Green Earth and various Seva Camps are organised on regular basis in order to inculcate the basic ethics like social responsibility among the students. 
  • Round the clock computing, internet facilities and latest software equipped labs are available.
  • Institute maintains an automated library with a subscription to online services like IEEE, Computer & Applied Science Journals, DELNET, INFLIBNET, OPAC (Open Public Access Catalogue).
